TRIATHLON

Description:

1. A team should consist of a maximum of 3 members.


2. Round 1: (Preliminary)
  General Aptitude
  No. of Questions: 15
  Time duration: 10 minutes
  15 General aptitude question based on quant and verbal will
be given, top 10 teams will qualify to next round.


3. Round 2: (Pre- Finals)
  Technical Aptitude
  No. of Questions: 15
  Time duration: 10 minutes
  15 Technical aptitude question based on latest trends and
programming language will be given, top 6 teams will qualify
to next round.

4. Round 3: (Finals)
  Treasure Hunt
  A C\C++ program will be given to all finalists. Output of the
program will lead them to a particular place, who reaches
the place first will be the winner.
